Getting liquidity in DeFi shouldn’t mean giving up your position. **Avana** lets users borrow against their LP positions, turning active liquidity into productive collateral.

Instead of forcing liquidity providers to withdraw, sell, or unwind their positions, Avana unlocks borrowing power directly from AMM liquidity. LPs can keep earning fees while accessing liquidity when they need it.

## Unlocking LP Positions as Collateral

At its core, Avana redefines how liquidity positions are used across DeFi. LP positions are no longer just passive assets — they become collateral that can support borrowing, leverage, and more capital-efficient strategies.

Avana is designed to make LP-backed lending safer, smarter, and easier to use through:

- **LP positions as collateral**
- **Borrowing without unwinding liquidity**
- **Capital-efficient lending markets**
- **Automated collateral and risk management**
- **Composable DeFi infrastructure**
- **Open-source protocol development**

By turning AMM liquidity into usable collateral, Avana helps liquidity providers do more with the positions they already hold.

## Why Avana?

Liquidity providers should not have to choose between earning trading fees and accessing capital.

With Avana, LPs can remain active in liquidity markets while unlocking borrowing power from their positions. This creates a more efficient DeFi experience where liquidity is not trapped, idle, or difficult to use.

Avana is building a new credit layer for DeFi — one where LP positions become a core collateral primitive for borrowing and capital efficiency.
